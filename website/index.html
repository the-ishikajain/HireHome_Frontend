Frequently Asked Questions

~What items are provided in an Apartment?

All Apartments are fully stocked to ensure you have the comforts of home during your stay. This includes toiletries, fresh towels, tea, coffee, and kitchen essentials. We also provide a limited number of cleaning materials, cleaning equipment and laundry pods.

~How safe are the apartments?

In a nutshell, very safe. We have 24/7 surveillance at all apartments. This is for company and guest safety. Appropriate signage and usage of surveillance equipment is in accordance with GDPR laws.

~Do you provide daily housekeeping service?

We don’t provide daily housekeeping, but if needed, you can request extra towels, sheets, pillows, or coffee. Booking over 14 days do include a weekly clean with fresh linen and towels.

~What happens if my property is damaged by a guest?

Property owners can request damage deposits from guests. Deposits can help cover any potential damage that a guest might cause, providing assurance that your property will be treated respectfully. If anything does go wrong, it can be reported to our team through our misconduct reporting feature.
